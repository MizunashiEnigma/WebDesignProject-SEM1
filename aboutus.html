About Us


Who are We?

We are PlayBy. A company dedicated to tabletop enjoyment. We've come together from a shared passion for the tabletop gaming and the many stories we have as a result of playing these games.

Don't worry we won't roleplay, just for you.
This is our marketing team, who definitely wrote this.
What We Do

Our mission is to be a introductory force and positive influence in the world of Tabletop Gaming. We want to introduce people to the communities they may not have been a part of in the first place.
This is mission critical for us. It is our wish for more stories to be create. Stories are where memories go when they're forgotten.
